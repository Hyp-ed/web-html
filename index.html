When hours become minutes

We are HypED. A team of 50 students from the University of Edinburgh’s Engineering, Economic and Business Schools. We are collaborating to develop Hyperloop and build the future of transportation.

Learn more



keyboard_arrow_down







The HypED pod introduces advanced new technologies to offer a faster, safer and more comfortable experience. The pod has been designed from the ground up to employ renewable energy solutions and further the advances in autonomous travel. This is Hyperloop.



keyboard_arrow_down

people

Designed for people

The sleek, modern interior provides comfort and serenity for travel. Modular seating and a fast pod-loading system offers revolutionary solutions to any form of mass transport.


volume_off

Smooth and silent

‘Magnetic Levitation’ and electric propulsion allows the pod to glide seamlessly and quickly through the low-pressure tube without making a sound.

flash_on

Powered by electricity

Hyperloop is supercharged by electric propulsion allowing for rapid acceleration. The pod produces zero carbon emissions, utilising clean, sustainable technologies including renewable energy.


settings

Autonomous

Hyperloop is fully autonomous, implementing a high factor of safety and aiding in maximising the pod’s speed, stability and performance throughout your journey.
