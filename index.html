What is Hyperloop?


Hyperloop is Elon Musk’s concept for the future of mass transportation. The Hyperloop is a new way of transporting people and goods quickly, safely, efficiently and sustainably.




Hyperloop utilises electric propulsion to accelerate a passenger or cargo pod through a low-pressure tube. The autonomous pod levitates slightly above the the track, gliding through the tube at speeds greater than an airline over long distances. Carbon emissions, noise, delays, weather concerns and pilot error are all eliminated with Hyperloop.




The HypED pod introduces advanced new technologies to offer a faster, safer and more comfortable experience. The pod has been designed from the ground up to employ renewable energy solutions and further the advances in autonomous travel. This is Hyperloop.



people

Designed for people

The sleek, modern interior provides comfort and serenity for travel. Modular seating and a fast pod-loading system offers revolutionary solutions to any form of mass transport.


volume_off

Smooth and silent

‘Magnetic Levitation’ and electric propulsion allows the pod to glide seamlessly and quickly through the low-pressure tube without making a sound.

flash_on

Powered by electricity

Hyperloop is supercharged by electric propulsion allowing for rapid acceleration. The pod produces zero carbon emissions, utilising clean, sustainable technologies including renewable energy.


settings

Autonomous

Hyperloop is fully autonomous, implementing a high factor of safety and aiding in maximising the pod’s speed, stability and performance throughout your journey.

The HypED team is entered in two international competitions, with the aim of revolutionising the mass transport industry through the development of Hyperloop. Over the next year we will devise a business case study for Hyperloop in the UK as part of the ‘Hyperloop One Global Challenge’. The engineering team will design and build a fully functioning pod for use in SpaceX’s ‘Hyperloop Pod Competition’ and compete against universities from around the world, to create the fastest and most innovative pod.
